    Installation Procedure

    1. 1]The authorized DSoft representative will visit the subscriber's premises to carry-out service demo.
    2. 2]The subscriber is required to pay ‘Subscription Amount’ as mentioned in the plan details on installation post the service demo at the installation location to the authorized DSoft installation representative only.
    3. 3]The authorized DSoft installation representative will carry a DSoft ID card. The subscriber can ask the DSoft representative for valid Government ID like PAN card or driving license to verify his identification.
    4. 4]DSoft will not be responsible for any payment made by the subscriber to an unauthorized person claiming to be a DSoft representative.
    5. 5]Service installation will be done within 3 working days from the date of realisation of subscription amount.
    6. In case of cheque payment, service installation shall take 6-10 working days from the cheque’s submission date.
    7. 6]Post successful installation, the DSoft representative will provide Subscriber Registration Form (SRF), which needs to be filled with complete and correct information.

      8. The following documents need to be submitted along with the SRF (if not submitted on website).:-

      >>>>Valid Proof of Identity (self-attested A4 size photocopy)

      >>>>Valid Proof of Address (self-attested A4 size photocopy)

      >>>>>One passport size color photograph

    8. 6]The subscriber will receive a User ID on his/her registered Email ID and registered mobile number. After the completion of installation, DSoft representative will assist the subscriber in generating the DSoft password through DSoft service login page.
    9. 7]Do not share your User ID and Password with anyone, including DSoft representatives.

    Bills And Payments

    ****.

  • DSoft Service bill delivery is done as per the option registered by the subscriber:

  • eCopy:- Only e-bill shall be sent to the registered Email ID.

  • Physical copy: The physical bill shall be sent to the registered billing address.

  • Additionally, e-bill shall be sent to the registered Email ID.

  • Kindly ensure that bills are paid on time to enjoy seamless service.

  • The Service Bill can be paid in 4 convenient ways:-
  • 1. My DSoft App - helps you to make payments for your account on the go
  • 2. Online Payment - Net Banking or Credit/Debit Card
  • 3. Cash Payment
  • 4. Cheque / Demand Draft

  • In case a subscriber has paid the Service bill through Cheque/Demand Draft without mentioning 10 Digit User ID/ Service ID/ Billing Account Number (BAN) on it, the amount will not get credited to the respective subscriber account.

  • For payment verification, please contact DSoft Care @ 020-27800000 and provide your cheque number, User ID, Service ID, BAN, details of cheque drop box, details of collection executive, if cheque is collected.
